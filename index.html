What is the combined ratio calculator?
The combined ratio calculator is a tool that measures an insurance company's combined ratio.
How to calculate combined ratio?
To calculate combined ratio you should use the following formula:
combined ratio = ((claim loss + loss adjustments + under writing expense) / premiums) * 100
Why use combined ratio calculator?
The combined ratio calculator is used in order to determine an insurance company's combined ratio.
